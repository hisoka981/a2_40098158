Here are some useful tips for a first-time dog owner

1) It is easier to train dogs earlier than later. We either recommend hiring a dog trainer for this or looking up some resources online to do it yourself.

2) Different dog breeds require different levels of activity. It is important to figure this out early on, and we recommend finding for online forums for this, or ask one of our employees at the shelter.

3) Unlike cats, dogs aren't too great at cleaning themselves so we recommend the ocassional showers, however the frequency depends on the breed.

4) Lots of dogs require daily walks but besides this, it is also important for some dogs to socialize with other dogs. This is highly dependent on the breed so we recommend either asking one of our employees or look for some resources online.

5) If you are a first-time dog owner, do not hesitate to ask questions. We at Luigi's shelter are here to help!
