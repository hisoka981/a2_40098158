Luigi's Cat And Dog Shelter

Luigi's Cat And Dog Shelter Has Been Operating Since 2010

Luigi, the owner of this shelter, has been taking care of cats and dogs from his home for decades. Luigi has nursed and taken care of dozens of cats and dogs, however, he recently found himself being spread too thin so he decided to open a shelter which is now located at 3438 Crescent street.

Luigi's shelter opened up during the summer of 2010, and since then the shelter has seen over 100 dogs and cats.

If you are looking for to provide a loving home to a dog or cat, feel free to browse our available pets and fill out some forms under the find a dog/cat tab to get started!
